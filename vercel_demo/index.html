From alert noise to a usable incident narrative in one place.

OnCallAI is a prototype for DevOps and SRE teams that ingests alerts, analyzes logs and service context, recommends escalation, and generates explainable incident reports.

Why it matters

On-call teams lose time switching across alerts, logs, dashboards, and tribal knowledge.

Alert intake

Supports CloudWatch-style payloads, simulated alerts, and deduplicated repeated alarms.

Explainable RCA

Combines heuristic detection with retrieval-backed context to produce grounded findings.

Escalation guidance

Recommends who should be paged and why, based on severity, age, service tier, and repeat volume.

Operator workspace

Surfaces queue health, incident history, timeline steps, reports, and service context in one UI.

Demo flow

A simple, reliable workflow for walking through the product live.

1

Ingest alert

CloudWatch-style alarms are normalized and stored as incidents with alert metadata.

2

Collect context

The system pulls relevant logs and service enrichment such as owners, runbooks, and dashboards.

3

Analyze evidence

Heuristic rules and retrieved examples produce likely issue, root cause hints, and mitigations.

4

Recommend escalation

The app computes priority, paging target, and suggested next action for first responders.

5

Present the incident

A transparent dashboard shows queue signals, agent timeline, report output, and raw evidence.
